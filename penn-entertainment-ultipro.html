Penn Entertainment and UltiPro: Streamlining HR and Payroll

In the fast-paced world of entertainment and gaming, managing a large workforce efficiently is crucial for success. Companies like Penn Entertainment, a leading force in the industry, rely on robust Human Capital Management (HCM) systems to handle everything from payroll to employee benefits. One such system frequently associated with large organizations is UltiPro.

UltiPro, now part of UKG (Ultimate Kronos Group), is a comprehensive cloud-based HCM solution designed to help businesses manage their entire employee lifecycle. For an organization as extensive and geographically diverse as Penn Entertainment, which operates numerous casinos, racetracks, and related properties, implementing a powerful platform like UltiPro makes considerable sense.

So, what exactly does UltiPro offer to a company like Penn Entertainment? Its core functionalities typically include:

  • Payroll Processing: Ensuring employees are paid accurately and on time, handling taxes, deductions, and compliance.
  • HR Administration: Managing employee data, onboarding, offboarding, and compliance reporting.
  • Benefits Administration: Enrolling employees in health insurance, retirement plans, and other benefits.
  • Talent Management: Supporting recruitment, performance management, learning, and succession planning.
  • Time and Attendance: Tracking employee work hours, managing schedules, and integrating with payroll.
  • Reporting and Analytics: Providing insights into workforce data to support decision-making.

For Penn Entertainment employees, having access to an integrated system like UltiPro (or its UKG successor) often means a centralized portal for essential information. Employees can typically log in to check their pay stubs, view and enroll in benefits, request time off, update personal information, and access company policies. This self-service capability reduces the administrative burden on HR departments and empowers employees with convenient access to their data.

From a business perspective, implementing a system like UltiPro helps Penn Entertainment achieve greater operational efficiency, reduce manual errors, ensure compliance with labor laws, and gain better visibility into workforce trends. Managing payroll for thousands of employees across different locations with varying pay rules is a complex task that a sophisticated HCM system is built to handle.

In conclusion, the use of a powerful platform like UltiPro allows Penn Entertainment to streamline critical HR and payroll processes. This not only supports the company's extensive operations but also provides employees with a user-friendly interface to manage their employment details, contributing to a more efficient and engaged workforce within the dynamic entertainment industry.
